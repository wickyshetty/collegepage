KLE's BCA College, Bangalore

“Performance leads to recognition. Recognition brings respect. Respect enhances power. Humility and grace in one’s moments of power enhances dignity of an organization”- Narayanmurthy, former CEO, Infosys.

  The year 2000 was a remarkable era for the IT industry, wherein there was a huge requirement of skilled software professionals, since its inception, the college has been a hub of activities. A lot of emphasis is given on learning outside the curriculum thus it can be termed as “Technology learning enrichment centre”.

   We encourage different approaches to teaching and learning that are both innovative and relevant to an increasingly global academic environment. The Cultural Diversity sets out the college's commitment to an institutional culture in which all students and staff share an appreciation of cultural diversity in outlook, values, and understanding.

  We provide the opportunity of interaction between the students and the representatives of Industry to facilitate placement in suitable jobs for all those who pass out from our College. We have an excellent placement record and most students have their careers charted out well before they complete graduation. Campus interview facilities are arranged by the institute. We provide guidance, training and facilitation to prepare the students for the challenges ahead in career. We aim at cent percent placements for our students.

  We provide quality sustained essential education at all times adapting to changing environment for a meaningful and self-supporting life.



Dr. Prabhakar B Kore, MP Chancellor-KLE University Chairman-KLE Society
